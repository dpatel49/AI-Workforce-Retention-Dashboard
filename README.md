# AI-Workforce-Retention-Dashboard

## Project Overview
An AI-powered workforce analytics dashboard that helps 
companies understand why employees leave and how salary, 
satisfaction, and overtime affect employee retention.

## Tools Used
- Excel — Data cleaning and pivot table analysis
- Tableau — Exploratory visual analysis
- Power BI — Interactive dashboard with KPIs
- AI Insights — Risk categorization and recommendations

## Dataset
IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
- 1,470 employees
- 35 features including Age, Department, Salary, 
  Overtime, Job Satisfaction, Attrition

## Key Findings
- Overall Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Sales department has highest attrition
- Overtime employees are 3x more likely to leave
- Low satisfaction employees show highest exit rates
- Young employees (18-27) leave most frequently

## Workforce Risk Analysis
| Risk Category | Employees | Percentage |
|---|---|---|
| High Risk | 155 | 10.29% |
| Medium Risk | 708 | 46.98% |
| Low Risk | 644 | 42.73% |

## Dashboard Pages
1. Main Dashboard — KPIs, charts, slicers
2. AI Workforce Insights — Risk alerts, 
   recommendations, key findings

## AI Recommendations
- Sales: Reduce overtime and improve retention bonuses
- R&D: Career development and salary reviews
- HR: Share best practices across departments
